About Me

Once again, I am a senior at Penn State Behrend majoring in Digital Media.
With a minor in Marketing, I hope to create promotional content and media for business and their services.

My personal interests include sports, video games and music. As a New Jersey kid, I am a fan of all of the New York Sports teams. I also have a jersey collection that includes former New York Knicks guard RJ Barret, and former New York Yankees infielder Gleyber Torres. My favorite sports memory was being in attendance at the 2022 American League Division Series between the New York Yankees and Cleveland Guardians. In the first inning, Giancarlo Stanton launched a two run home run to get the game going. The stadium was rocking the rest of the game, resulting in the Yankees advancing to the next round of the playoffs.

My favorite music artists include Drake, Lil Uzi Vert, and Future. I am a big fan of hip-hop and rap, but specially I enjoy the sub-category of trap music. The beat and melodies have always been a favorite of mine and has kept me attached for several years now. Additionally, I love going to concerts. My first concert was my freshman year of college when I went to see A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie in Philadelphia. Over the past four years, I have seen A Boogie (twice), Drake, Travis Scott, Lil Yatchy and Babytron.
